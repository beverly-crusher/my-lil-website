Stardate

Format

Solamani Stardates are formatted:

Year.Month.Week.Day.Hour:Minute

A year consists of 8 months of 45 days broken into 5 weeks of 9 Earth-days.
1.1.1.1.00:00 corresponds to the implementation of the Solamani Accord.

Usage

The Stardate is used throughout the Solar Subsector and in some of the adjoining parts of neighboring subsectors: Vega, Esperance, Dingir, Capella, Gemini, Kukulkan, Arcturus, and Banasdan in galaxy-wise (counter-clockwise) order from the coreward.
