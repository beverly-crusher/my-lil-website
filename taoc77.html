8 Hearthfire

I left Pelgiad early and headed to Vivec. The road snakes south along one of the fingers of the archipelago, winding through farmland until Vivec looms into view, massive stone and adobe ziggurats rising from the bay. The ziggurat I entered through had the Imperial standard plastered on its faces. I learned from the residents that that "canton" as they are referred to, is the foreign quarter. Each of the great houses has a dedicated Canton, plus a foreigners canton and two devoted to residence.
