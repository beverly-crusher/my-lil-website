Obviously, I passed that tunnel, and continued on to the "Queen's Chamber" - marked so in both Dunmeris and Cyrodiilic - the majority of the workers are humans.

I entered the chamber, and saw two people on the other side, one carrying a large burlap sack full of eggs. They both had the same tools as the other miners, and rushed me with them as soon as I entered. I drank one of Ajira's potions, a

draught of flame shield I had been fingering since I saw the first Kwama Worker, as they ran at me across the chamber. I tried to shout at them to stop, to reason with them, but they reacted only with violence. Between my suit of armor and the flame shield, I killed them with almost no trouble: one bashed my shield arm with the broad end of the tool - it's not broken, but I do have a very large bruise. Gauntlets and pauldrons will be my next purchases.
