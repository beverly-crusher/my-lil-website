Heather

Mostly the same as the Heather on the continent, this variety grows much closer to the ground. Appears to have the same properties as continental Heather, although I have been unable to reproduce a confidence potion due to a lack of complimenting ingredients as of yet - but it seems to have the same uses.

Kreshweed

Large plant that seems to prefer to grow in drier, rockier soil. Composed of a mass of long, broad basal leaves that are full of very strong fibers - I imagine rope could be

produced from them. It sends up flowering stalks that produce a large, single yellow cup-shaped flower with a large central style.

Alchemical properties: seems to have confidence-boosting properties, but fails to produce any potent results when brewed or distilled with Heather.
