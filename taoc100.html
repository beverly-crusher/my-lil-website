They are in desperate need of potions - they have plenty of ways to cure these sicknesses, but they do not have the means to produce them in large enough quantity to also tend to their herds. Judging from the skins hung in the wise woman's hut, their traditional method of

producing curatives is through fermentation rather than distillation, a much more time-consuming process. They use a different set of ingredients to create potions to cure soul sickness than any recipes I have encountered among alchemists of the mages' guilds - Roobrush, two species of lichen, one dark green, the other almost black (the wise woman said they are interchangeable, but it is better to use both), Kagouti tripe, and a material similar to Scrib Jelly produced from Shalk (too bitter to eat, they said).
