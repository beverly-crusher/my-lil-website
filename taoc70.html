4 Hearthfire

I spent the morning in the market, selling my potions to the merchants and one passerby: an ill orsimer, a member of the imperial garrison here. He only bought one potion, though.

After the morning in the market, I headed south into a thick fog rolling in from the ocean. After nearly an hour of being lost save the preservation of my cardinal direction, I encountered a fortress, seemingly abandoned from the outside. I entered to ask for directions, and encountered a dremora!
