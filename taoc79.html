While I was looking for the inn, I found the alchemist's shop mentioned in the pamphlet. The shop is owned by a breton woman and is immaculately neat and tidy, all ingredients measured and individually packaged for ease of use. I showed her the pamphlet and traded some ingredients I had gathered along the northern end of the island. She suspects a rival is producing the pamphlets.

I have since found the inn, eaten, and rented a room for the night.
