25 of Last Seed

I spent the morning planning my route back - the path I had taken snaked around a spider-y ridge, and I decided I could cut the distance in half by hiking up to the top of the ridge and following the ridge back to the dwemer ruin.

I followed the draw I had camped in toward the ridge. I happened upon a camp of two dunmer. I shouted a greeting, and saw one of them nock an arrow. I darted behind a boulder.
