22 of Last Seed

I woke early, and hiked back to Balmora. Azura be blessed for holding off the rain until I had reached the city. I returned the flower samples to Ajira, who promptly sent me on an errand to buy a new mixing bowl. I returned to find her incensed: her colleague had gotten her back for the fake soul gem and hidden her reports, and Ajira did not turn out to be a good sport. I helped her look for them, and read the one I found before I returned it to her - not good scholarship, but the four potions she gave me as a thanks are of excellent quality - she clearly knows her craft, but not her field.
