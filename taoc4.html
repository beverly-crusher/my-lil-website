I decided to poke around in the swamp next to the town to find some ingredients for potions - no luck: I do not recognize any species of plant or fungus I have encountered so far. I will have to relearn everything I know. The traveler's guide I bought has later has helped me to identify some of these ingredients, but their properties I can only guess at.

While gathering mushrooms, I found the entrance to the cave the two Altmer had mentioned. I allowed myself in, intending to play the fool, but a Dunmer shouted a threat and charged me with a dagger no sooner than I had opened the door. I bolted back out, and they

did not pursue me further.
