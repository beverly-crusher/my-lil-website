Violet Coprinus

Produces a tall, teacup-shaped head on a tall stalk, brown on the edges and bottom, transitioning to light blue in the center with white spots throughout. Produces brilliantly purple spores that fluoresce the same at night. The travellers guide lists it as deadly poisonous.

Alchemical properties: unwilling to test on self, need alternative means to do so.
