Willow Flower

Small understory plants that produce a cluster of long, thin basal leaves and several flowering stalks

with large red-pink five-petaled flowers with white centers. The flowers produce a lot of pollen.

Alchemical properties: the pollen produces great restorative effects, and a tea made of the anthers (in addition to things that actually taste good - the practice seems to be to add the anthers to any tea and tout it as a curative) is commonly sold in inns.
