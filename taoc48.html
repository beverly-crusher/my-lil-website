26 of Last Seed

After sleeping late, I went back to the Mages Guild to get more work - Ranis told me to intimidate an unlicensed healer in the South Wall Corner Club into ceasing business. Pretty unsavory.

He's the one who has been providing the publican with a supply of healing potions in addition to his normal fare. He is also clearly working under protection of the Theives' Guild - I let him know that Ranis sent me to intimidate him and he thanked me. The Mages' Guild charges for healing, the Temple charges for healing; good to have somebody in town who charges

a little less. Besides, I feel safe lying to Ranis knowing that Ranis knows he's a Thieves' Guild agent.

Ranis didn't buy my story, but obviously didn't care. A scholar had stopped in town and was looking for an escort to Pelgiad. Ranis wants his notes, and insinuated I might kill him along the way to get them. By Azura, I detest that woman.

I chatted with some of the other members of the guild - Estirdalin told me a Larrius Varro from Fort Moonmoth had been poking around town looking for me.
