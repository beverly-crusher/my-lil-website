A thunderstorm ended my invasion of these insects' home however, and I scurried along the road, ducking under the entryway of Fort Moonmoth to attempt to wait out the storm. The guard shooed me along after a while, I would estimate an hour and a half, and I continued to Balmora through the heavy downpour.

The southern road leading into Balmora crosses very nearly underneath the Silt Strider dock, and I was once again awestruck by the size of these creatures: outlandish looking insects with no recognizable features save their appendages: their heads are incredibly small compared to the

rest of their bodies and nestled under layers of chitin sheathing.

The difference in architectural styles is dizzying: Balmora is a Dunmer city, not an imperial settlement, and the buildings are a labyrinth of interconnected cube-shaped adobes. Entire blocks share walls with each other, and it seems that separate households live in both the upstairs and downstairs of each house.
