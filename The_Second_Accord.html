The Second Accord

The Second Accord is the treaty following the surrender of Solamani High Command to The Second at the Battle of Sol on stardate 2301.6.5.1.

It incorporates the Solamani Accord into The First, established the Solamani host quota, extends official use of the Credit into the Solar Subsector, established hierarchical stratification of the Solamani Accord within The First, and dissolved the Solamani military.
