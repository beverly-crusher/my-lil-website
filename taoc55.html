A while later, we encountered a Guar-herder, shepherding a flock of perfectly docile, if not outgoing and friendly, guar - something like ten in number. They don't appear to have any kind of discernible sexual dimorphism, but saurids are very far out of my realm of confident knowledge.

Eventually, the road lost elevation until it began to twist through the swampy bitter coast.
