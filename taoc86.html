My plan is to travel to Sadrith Mora along the coast, stopping at Azura’s temple along the way.

I am spending the night in a spare room at the Guild. I paid for a bath at the Council Club - expensive, but I no longer smell like ash.

12 Hearthfire

An uneventful day. I hired a silt-strider to take me to Vivec, and we made it here shortly after nightfall. The features of the landscape look surreally small from the back of a silt-strider; the perspective it lends is meditative and stirs ones mind.

I am in an inn in the foreigners’ canton, and had a light supper of soup and bread.
