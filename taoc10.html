He was a very interesting person - Dunmeris was not his first language and nor is it mine, so our conversation was a little limited. He told me he is an Ashlander, and that he has come to Pelgiad to make his fortune as a mercenary. He seemed proud of his success thus far, not that it seems surprising - he was easily a full head taller than me. He told me the ash that blows off of Red Mountain is blighted, and makes you sick, insane, and haunted by nightmares.
