31 of Last Seed

I spent the entire evening of the 30th around town. For a dunmer with a reputation as a merry-maker and gambler, he is certainly inconspicuous. I found his tracks everywhere in the form of five different merchants that he owed money to, but no sign of the dancing food that trod them. I returned groggily (and a little drunk) to the Ald Skar Inn nearly at midnight, frustrated but in high spirits nonetheless: the architecture of the city is lovely despite it seeming bizarre to me.
