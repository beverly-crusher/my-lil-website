I have spent the remainder of the evening cleaning the inside of the temple: I found two old brooms and a stockpile of charcoal for the braziers, as well a bundle of candles. I left the candles alone, but cleaned and refilled the braziers and swept the entrance to the hall. I cooked one of my two remaining kwama eggs on the beach, and have found a warm spot by a brazier in the temple for my bedroll.

15 Hearthfire

I left the temple in the morning, heading north up the peninsula along the trail that Yakum had marked for me in Pelgiad. The trail skirted dangerously close to a temple full of atronachs, but I slipped by unnoticed. I wonder what kind of social structure exists among a garrison of atronachs. I have heard they are unliving golems in this plane, an extension of the summoner's will.
