Roobrush

A low-lying shrub with woody stems and small, waxy leaves. Seems to prefer poor soil. Produces clusters of tiny, six-petaled white flowers that mature into hard dark-red or brown fruits. Has a distinct sweet and oily smell.

Alchemical properties: laxative. Poisonous leaves.

Slough Fern

A large fern, grows at the foots of trees. The central fronds curl tightly into themselves and seem to produce more spores than the basal fronds, resulting in a "head" or "fruit" full of spores.

Alchemical properties: the spores produce numbing and deadening effects - could induce paralysis at higher concentration?
